What is financial literacy?

Have you all heard the story of "The Ant and the Grasshopper"? Well if you haven't then please do because the Ant in the story is going to give you some amazing finance management goals! The Ant in the tale is financially intellectual enough to store the grain for bitter winter days. Yes, this is what being financially literate means, you have your money when you need it the most. It is your ability to make sane judgments while spending money so that you face no resistance in managing and growing your bucks. Financial literacy will help you to create a roadmap for yourself, using which you can make sound earning and spending decisions.
